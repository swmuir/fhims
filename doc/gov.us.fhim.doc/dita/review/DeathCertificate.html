DeathCertificate Class

A document issued by a medical practitioner certifying the deceased state of a person. In the United States, this is typically the U.S. Standard Certificate of Death (Rev. 11/2003).

DeathCertificate.pregnancyStatusCategory

[US Standard Certificate Of Death Field 36]. If the decedent is female, this property provides an indication of whether she was pregnant at or near the time of death. This information is important in determining pregnancy-related mortality. Possible values include: Not pregnant within past year, Pregnant at time of death, Not pregnant, but pregnant within 42 days of death, Not pregnant, but pregnant 43 days to 1 year before death, Unknown if pregnant within the past year.
